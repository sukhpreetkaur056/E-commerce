Fashion Tips Based on Body Type

Visual styling guides tailored to different body shapes

Hourglass

Balance your curves by highlighting your waist. Fitted tops, pencil skirts, and wrap dresses enhance your silhouette beautifully.

Apple

Elongate your figure with flowy tunics, V-necklines, and straight-leg pants. A-line skirts draw attention away from the midsection.

Pear

Balance your shape by wearing bright or structured tops. A-line skirts and bootcut pants flatter your lower half.

Rectangle

Create curves with peplum tops, belts, and ruffles. Layered pieces add dimension and shape to your frame.

Inverted Triangle

Soften shoulders with scoop necklines and flowy tops. Wide-leg trousers and A-line skirts add proportion to your lower half.
