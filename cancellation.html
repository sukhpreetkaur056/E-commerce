Cancellation Policy

Our Cancellation Policy

At The Style Seed, we understand that sometimes you may need to cancel an order. We strive to process your orders quickly, so please review our cancellation policy carefully.

You may request to cancel your order as long as it has not yet been shipped. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled and will need to be processed as a return or exchange according to our Returns & Exchanges policy.

How to Cancel Your Order

To request a cancellation, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to your account: Go to our website and log in to your registered account.
  2. Navigate to Order History: Find the "Order History" or "My Orders" section.
  3. Locate the order: Find the specific order you wish to cancel.
  4. Request Cancellation: If the order status allows cancellation (i.e., it hasn't been shipped), you should see an option to "Cancel Order" or a similar button. Click on this option and follow the on-screen instructions.
  5. Confirmation: Once your cancellation request is submitted, you will receive an email confirmation regarding the status of your cancellation.

If you do not see a cancellation option or if you are unable to cancel through your account, please Contact Us immediately with your order number and cancellation request. We will do our best to accommodate your request if the order has not yet been processed for shipping.

Important Notes Regarding Cancellations

  • While we will make every effort to accommodate cancellation requests, we cannot guarantee cancellation if the order has already been processed for shipment.
  • If your cancellation is successful, any applicable refunds will be processed according to our Refund Policy. Please allow [Number] business days for the refund to reflect in your account.
  • For orders paid using certain payment methods, there might be a slight delay in the refund processing time, which is beyond our control.
  • The cancellation of custom-made or personalized items may not be possible once production has begun. Please check the specific terms and conditions for such items at the time of purchase.
