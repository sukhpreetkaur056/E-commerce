Returns & Exchanges

Our Returns & Exchanges Policy

At The Style Seed, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not entirely happy with your order, we're here to help. Please read our policy below regarding returns and exchanges.

You can return or exchange items within 14 days of receiving your order.

Conditions for Returns & Exchanges

To be eligible for a return or exchange, please ensure that the following conditions are met:

  • The item(s) must be in their original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags and packaging intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or invoice) must be provided.
  • Certain items, such as [Specify any non-returnable items, e.g., lingerie, swimwear due to hygiene reasons], are not eligible for return or exchange unless they are faulty.
  • Items purchased during a sale or with a discount code may have specific return/exchange conditions. Please refer to the terms of the sale.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account: Visit our website and log in to your registered account.
  2. Navigate to Order History: Go to the "Order History" or "My Orders" section.
  3. Select the order: Find the order containing the item(s) you wish to return or exchange.
  4. Request Return/Exchange: If the option is available, click on "Return Item(s)" or "Exchange Item(s)" and follow the on-screen instructions. You will need to specify the item(s) and the reason for the return/exchange. For exchanges, please indicate the desired replacement item (size, color, etc.).
  5. Confirmation: Once your request is submitted, you will receive an email with further instructions, including a return shipping label if applicable.
  6. Packaging: Carefully package the item(s) you are returning or exchanging, ensuring they are well-protected. Include a copy of your order confirmation or the return/exchange form (if provided).
  7. Shipping: Ship the package back to us using the provided shipping label or the instructions in the email.

If you do not see a return/exchange option in your order history, please Contact Us with your order number and details of the item(s) you wish to return or exchange.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size, color, or a different product of the same value, please indicate this in your return/exchange request. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

If the desired exchange item is of a higher value, you will be required to pay the difference. If it's of a lower value, a partial refund will be processed according to our Refund Policy.

We will ship the exchanged item to you once we have received and processed your returned item.
